240613

Well that was probably the most interesting WWDC in a while. Not saying it was great or perfect, but definitely a turning point. That intro was amazing though! Here’s what caught my eye along with my gut reaction:

iOS

  • Custom home screen icon placement - Wow, finally.
  • Custom home screen icon tints - Hmm, not quite right.
  • Ability to lock apps - Nice, but also sherlocks this as a paid app feature.
  • Ability to hide apps - Sus stuff aside, hiding utility apps is a win.
  • iMessage RCS support - Finally.
  • iMessage rich text - Oh cool.
  • iMessage emoji tapbacks - Finally. Yes. Perfect.
  • iMessage scheduled texts - Finally. Yes. Perfect.
  • iMessage over satelite - Oh cool.
  • Airpods shake/nod for accept/decline - Oh cool. That won’t misfire at all…

watchOS

  • Live activities - Oh cool. Also cool that it reuses dynamic island activities.
  • Vitals app - Oh cool, hopefully useful.

  • Smart script for cleaning hand writing, pasting, and spell checking - Oh wow, yes! My hand writing is miserable.
